Chevrolet Camaro IROC-Z

1981-1992

145 hp

The third-generation Chevrolet Camaro was introduced for the 1982 model year by Chevrolet. It continued to use General Motors's F-body platform and produced a "20th Anniversary Commemorative Edition" for 1987 and "25th Anniversary Heritage Edition" for 1992. These were also the first Camaros with factory fuel injection, four-speed automatic transmissions, five-speed manual transmissions, four-cylinder engines, 16-inch wheels, and hatchback bodies.
